Welcome · first run

Before you build a world

The settings can look a little confronting at first — lots of knobs, locks, and options. We designed them for simple controls: change what you care about, leave the rest alone, or hit Randomize and then only edit what bothers you.

That said: these settings matter. Genre, tone, death rules, how NPCs behave, magic, and checks all teach the model what kind of story this is. You’re not filling paperwork for its own sake — you’re shaping how the world will play.

The next screens walk each important control with a spotlight. Take your time — use Back anytime.
